## Full Description (max 4000 chars)

Planning a group trip? Vacationist makes it effortless.

Whether you're organising a weekend escape with friends, a family holiday, or a multi-stop adventure with colleagues, Vacationist brings everyone onto the same page — in real time.

**🗳️ Decide together, not alone**
Suggest activities and let the whole group vote. Thumbs up, thumbs down, or block an idea entirely. Voting closes automatically and the group's favourites rise to the top. No more endless WhatsApp polls.

**💸 Expense splitting, done right**
Log every expense — hotel, dinner, taxi — assign a payer, and choose how to split: equally, by percentage, or as a fixed amount. The built-in balance tracker always shows who owes what. Settle up and move on.

**🛒 Shared shopping lists**
Create lists your whole group can edit in real time. Check items off as you shop. Perfect for packing lists, grocery runs, and group gift buying.

**📝 Shared notes**
A living notepad for the trip — addresses, phone numbers, local tips, whatever the group needs to remember.

**🏨 Accommodation tracking**
Keep all hotel and rental details in one place: booking reference, check-in times, address. Everyone always has it.

**✈️ Flight & transfer details**
Log every flight and transfer so the group knows when and where to be. No more "what time does your flight land?"

**🔒 Encrypted travel documents**
Store your passport or ID card details inside the app, protected by biometric authentication and end-to-end encryption. Grant temporary access to a travel companion when needed — revoke it any time.

**🔔 Smart notifications**
Get notified when a teammate suggests an activity, logs an expense, or the group reaches a vote decision. Stay in the loop without being overwhelmed.

**👥 Invite by link**
Share a single invite link — no accounts required to join. Guests can participate in votes and view the trip right away.

---

Built with privacy in mind: your travel documents are encrypted with pgcrypto and never stored in plain text. Push notifications are handled via Expo's secure push service.

Open an account with Google or a magic link — no passwords to remember.
