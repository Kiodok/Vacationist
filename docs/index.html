Common questions

Everything you need to know.

Vacationist is a free trip app for collaborative group travel planning. You can suggest and vote on activities, split expenses, track vacation spending, manage accommodations, share shopping lists, and keep everyone in sync — all in one place. It works for everything from city breaks to multi-week road trips.

Log any group cost — dinner, accommodation, transport, or anything else — choose who paid, and split equally or with custom amounts. Vacationist automatically tracks balances so everyone knows exactly who owes whom. Settle individual splits or clear everything at once when the trip ends.

Absolutely. Vacationist is ideal for van road trips and overland adventures. The shared calendar tracks each stop, the activity planner keeps the route flexible as plans evolve, the packing list keeps the van organized, and expense splitting makes shared fuel and campsite costs fair and transparent.

Sustainable travel starts with better coordination. When your group shares one vehicle instead of driving separately, books smarter, and avoids the waste of disorganized trips, the footprint shrinks. Vacationist helps you vote on lower-impact activities, coordinate shared transport, and plan more intentional travel.

Yes. Many Vacationist users coordinate timeshare stays, vacation rentals, and shared holiday properties. Track who's arriving when, split running costs among co-owners, manage shared shopping lists for the property, and keep everyone's travel documents accessible for check-in.

Each trip in Vacationist acts as a shared workspace. Invite anyone via a single link — they join instantly, no account needed to participate. The vacation tracker view shows your full trip timeline, off-calendar events, and spending at a glance. Changes sync in real time so everyone stays up to date.
