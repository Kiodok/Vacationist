What's inside

Everything your group needs,
nothing it doesn't.

Built for real trips with real people — messy schedules, split opinions, and all.

🗳️
Vote on activities
Suggest ideas and let the group decide — from "must do" to "group blocker". The best options rise naturally, with no endless debates.
💸
Split expenses fairly
Log every cost, choose who paid, and split equally or by custom amounts. Balances always show exactly who owes what.
🛒
Real-time shared lists
Shopping lists, packing checklists — anything the group tracks. Everyone adds and checks off items simultaneously, in real time.
✈️
Transfer & flights
Suggest flights, vote on options, record bookings, manage car rentals, and assign who rides with whom — all in one place.
🔒
Encrypted travel documents
Passport and ID details stored behind biometric authentication, AES-256 encrypted. Share temporary organiser access and revoke it any time.
🗓️
Shared calendar
A visual timeline of all planned activities, transfers, and check-ins — displayed in the trip's timezone automatically.
How it works

Ready in three steps.

1
Create a trip
Set your destination, pick your dates, and add a budget if you like. Ready in under a minute.
2
Invite your crew
Share a single link in any chat. Friends join instantly — no account sign-up required to participate.
3
Plan together
Vote on ideas, split costs, share lists and notes. Everyone in the loop, in real time.
