What's inside

Everything your group needs,
nothing it doesn't.

Built for real trips with real people — messy schedules, split opinions, and all.

🗳️
Vote on activities
Suggest what to do and let the group decide. Thumbs up, thumbs down, or block an idea entirely. The group's favourites rise to the top — no more endless polls.
💸
Split expenses fairly
Log every cost, choose who paid, and split equally, by percentage, or as fixed amounts. Built-in balances always show exactly who owes what.
🛒
Real-time shared lists
Shopping lists, packing lists, whatever you need. Everyone can add and check off items simultaneously — great for groceries, gifts, and gear runs.
📝
Group notes
A living notepad shared with the whole trip. Addresses, local tips, phone numbers — everything the group needs, always at hand.
🔒
Encrypted travel documents
Store your passport or ID card behind biometric authentication. End-to-end encrypted — we can't read it. Share temporary access to a travel companion, revoke any time.
🔗
Invite by link
Share one link and your crew is in. Guests can vote and view the trip right away — no account sign-up required to join.
